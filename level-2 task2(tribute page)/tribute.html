Tribute to Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system.

Timeline of Tesla's Life

  • 1856: Born in Smiljan, Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia).
  • 1884: Moves to the United States and begins working with Thomas Edison.
  • 1891: Patents the Tesla coil.
  • 1893: Demonstrates wireless power transmission.
  • 1943: Dies in New York City at the age of 86.

Major Achievements

  • Developed the alternating current (AC) electrical system.
  • Invented the Tesla coil.
  • Pioneered wireless communication and energy transmission.
  • Contributed to the development of X-rays.
  • Laid the groundwork for modern radio and television.
